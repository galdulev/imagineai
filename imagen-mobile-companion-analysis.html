Why Should We Build This?

The Strategic Opportunity

Photographers are inherently mobile professionals. They shoot on location, travel between events, and rarely sit at a desk during their busiest periods. Yet Imagen AI currently requires a desktop workflow‚Äîcreating a significant gap between when photos are captured and when they can be processed.

üí° The Core Insight

Photographers don't need to edit on mobile‚ÄîImagen AI handles that automatically. What they need is visibility, control, and client connection during the 85% of their day when they're away from their computer. The mobile companion isn't about replicating desktop functionality; it's about extending the Imagen workflow into the moments between shoots.
