## Products & Services (Detailed)

### 1. AI-Powered Photo Editing

**Core Functionality:**
- Consistent, accurate, and personalized photo editing in under 0.5s per photo
- Unlike presets, photos are individually edited according to their specific parameters
- Personal AI Profile that evolves and learns from your editing style

**How It Works:**
1. **Profile Training:** Upload 3,000-5,000 previously edited photos
2. **AI Learning:** Machine learning algorithms analyze your editing patterns
3. **Profile Creation:** A Personal AI Profile is generated based on your style
4. **Batch Editing:** Apply your profile to new photos automatically
5. **Refinement:** AI continues to learn and improve with feedback

**Editing Capabilities:**
| Category | Adjustments |
|----------|-------------|
| **Exposure** | Exposure, contrast, highlights, shadows, whites, blacks |
| **Color** | Temperature, tint, vibrance, saturation |
| **Tone Curve** | Highlights, lights, darks, shadows |
| **HSL** | Hue, saturation, luminance per color channel |
| **Split Toning** | Highlight and shadow color grading |
| **Sharpening** | Amount, radius, detail, masking |
| **Noise Reduction** | Luminance, color noise reduction |
| **Lens Corrections** | Profile corrections, chromatic aberration |
| **Transform** | Perspective corrections |

**Additional AI Tools:**
- **Crop & Straighten** — Automatic composition optimization
- **Subject Mask** — AI-powered subject detection and masking
- **Smooth Skin** — Intelligent skin retouching
- **Red-Eye Removal** — Automatic detection and correction

**Integration:**
- Adobe Lightroom Classic plugin
- Adobe Creative Cloud sync
- Standalone application available
- Non-destructive editing (XMP sidecar files)

---

### 2. AI Culling

**Core Functionality:**
- AI-powered photo selection that mimics human decision-making
- Identifies best photos based on technical quality and composition
- Reduces culling time from hours to minutes

**Selection Criteria:**
| Factor | What AI Analyzes |
|--------|------------------|
| **Sharpness** | Focus accuracy, motion blur detection |
| **Exposure** | Over/underexposure assessment |
| **Composition** | Subject positioning, framing |
| **Eyes** | Open eyes, eye contact, blink detection |
| **Expression** | Facial expressions, smiles |
| **Duplicates** | Similar shot grouping |
| **Technical Quality** | Noise, artifacts, lens issues |

**Workflow Features:**
- Culling and editing in the same application
- Customizable selection preferences and thresholds
- Preview photos with AI Profile applied during culling
- Original catalog remains intact
- Star ratings and color labels sync to Lightroom
- Group similar shots for easy comparison

**Output Options:**
- Mark as selected/rejected
- Apply star ratings (1-5 stars)
- Apply color labels
- Export selection to Lightroom
