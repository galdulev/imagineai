# Mobile Jobs to Be Done Research
## Wedding Photographer Mobile Workflow Analysis

**Strategic Research Document | Mobile Experience Gap**  
*Extracted from workflow analysis and 52 problems research*

---

## Executive Summary

This document identifies mobile-specific jobs to be done from the wedding photographer workflow and documents the critical inefficiencies photographers face without a great mobile experience. The analysis is based on the complete workflow breakdown and cross-referenced with the 52 problems identified in photographer research.

**Key Finding:** Wedding photographers have **zero mobile workflow integration** across the entire workflow—from lead management to social media marketing. This creates critical gaps in client communication (WhatsApp), payment collection, calendar management, social media posting (Instagram photos and Reels), and business operations.

**Total Mobile Jobs Identified:** 34 jobs across 5 workflow phases

---

## Mobile Jobs to Be Done: Wedding Day Workflow

### Phase: Wedding Day (8.5-13 hours total)

Based on the workflow analysis, photographers need to accomplish these jobs during the wedding day:

---

### Job 1: **Start Workflow During Breaks**
**When:** During 8-12 hour shoot, during breaks (30-60 min total break time)

**Current State:**
- Photographer shoots wedding (8-12 hours)
- Has 30-60 minutes of break time throughout the day
- Must wait until returning home to start any workflow
- No way to interact with Imagen AI during shoot

**Job to Be Done:**
> "When I have a break during the wedding day, I want to start culling or reviewing images so that I can make productive use of downtime and catch any issues early."

**Mobile Opportunity:**
- Start AI culling during breaks (saves 30-60 min per wedding)
- Preview images on mobile to catch issues early
- Begin workflow before returning home

**Time Impact:** 30-60 minutes saved per wedding

---

### Job 2: **Verify Backup Integrity Immediately**
**When:** After backup step (30-60 min), on-site

**Current State:**
- Photographer backs up memory cards to external drives
- Backup happens after returning home (or on-site with laptop)
- No way to verify backup integrity immediately
- Risk of data loss if backup fails

**Job to Be Done:**
> "When I finish backing up my memory cards, I want to verify the backup integrity immediately so that I know my photos are safe before I continue shooting or leave the venue."

**Mobile Opportunity:**
- Real-time backup verification on mobile
- Cloud backup status check
- Early detection of backup failures

**Risk Reduction:** Prevents data loss, provides peace of mind

---

### Job 3: **Share Preview with Client During Day**
**When:** Throughout wedding day, during breaks or after key moments

**Current State:**
