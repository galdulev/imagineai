# Slide 8.2: Visual Design & Presentation Approach
## Impact Model + Opportunity Mapping + Strategic Fit

---

## Design Philosophy

**Steve Jobs-Style Principles:**
- **One Big Idea Per Slide** - But this slide needs to show relationships
- **Visual Over Text** - Use diagrams, icons, color coding
- **Progressive Disclosure** - Could be 2-3 slides if needed
- **Clear Hierarchy** - What matters most is most prominent

---

## Recommended Approach: Split into 2-3 Slides

### Option A: Single Comprehensive Slide (If Must)
### Option B: Progressive Disclosure (Recommended) - 3 Slides

**I recommend Option B** - Better storytelling, less cognitive load, more impactful.

## Recommended: 3-Slide Sequence

**Why 3 slides?**
1. **Less cognitive load** - One concept at a time
2. **Better storytelling** - Builds narrative
3. **More impactful** - Each slide can be bold and focused
4. **Easier to present** - Natural pause points

**Flow:**
1. **8.2A:** "Here's how we think about opportunities" (Framework)
2. **8.2B:** "Here's how Gallery Delivery scores" (Deep dive)
3. **8.2C:** "Here's how it compares" (Matrix)
