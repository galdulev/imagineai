Mobile Experience Gap

What problem or inefficiency are they facing without a mobile experience?

Key Inefficiencies Without Mobile:
  • No Real-Time Upload During Events: Photographers can't upload photos during the wedding/event for immediate backup or preview. Must wait until they're back at their computer. Event photographers face 24-48 hour delivery expectations.
  • Can't Review Edits on the Go: After AI processing, photographers can't review edits while traveling or between shoots. They're tied to their desktop. 2-3 hours of travel time wasted per event.
  • Client Communication Delays: Can't respond to client messages, share previews, or check gallery status while away from computer. Creates communication gaps. 90%+ use external tools for client communication.
  • No On-Site Culling: Can't start culling process immediately after shoot while still on location. Wasted time during travel. 30-60 min of travel time could be productive.
  • Gallery Management Inefficiency: Can't manage client galleries, check delivery status, or handle client requests while mobile. Everything requires desktop access. 1-2 hours per job for gallery management.
  • Payment Collection Delays: Can't send invoices, check payment status, or follow up on payments while mobile. Delays cash flow. 85%+ use external payment tools.
  • Social Media Opportunity Loss: Can't quickly share behind-the-scenes content or previews to social media during events. Miss marketing moments. 80% post to social but manually.
  • Workflow Fragmentation: Forces photographers to be at their desk for every step. No flexibility for modern, mobile-first work style. 3-5 external tools required for mobile access.

Summary

We own 36% of the workflow (Culling & Editing). We've solved the most painful problems, but photographers still use 5-7 other tools for the rest of their workflow. 95%+ leave after editing.
Vision
Become the photographer one-stop-shop. Own the entire workflow from booking to delivery, maximizing Share of Workflow and LTV (+40% target).
Focus
Professional photographers, specifically wedding photographers. Highest pain (9/10), highest volume (2K-5K images), highest willingness to pay ($3K-$6K per wedding), strongest advocacy (40-50% of user base).
Next Steps
Prioritize gallery delivery, payment integration, and album design tools to extend workflow coverage and increase retention.
