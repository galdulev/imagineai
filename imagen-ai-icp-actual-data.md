## Validated ICP Profile

### Primary ICP: Wedding & Event Photographers

**Demographics:**
- Professional photographers (not hobbyists)
- Running a photography business
- Age 25-44 (based on industry data)
- Already using Adobe Lightroom

**Psychographics:**
- Time-constrained (editing is a bottleneck)
- Quality-conscious (won't sacrifice their style)
- Business-minded (want to scale)
- Tech-comfortable (willing to try AI)

**Behavioral:**
- Process 500-5,000+ photos per event
- Need turnaround in days, not weeks
- Want consistent style across galleries
- Spend 3-10+ hours editing per event currently

**Business Characteristics:**
- Solo photographers or small studios (1-5 people)
- Revenue: $50K-$500K annually (estimated)
- High season pressure (wedding season)
- Client-facing business

### Secondary ICP: Portrait & Commercial Photographers

**Similar characteristics but:**
- Lower volume per session
- Higher emphasis on style consistency
- More diverse client types
- Year-round workflow (less seasonal)

### Tertiary ICP: Real Estate Photographers

**Distinct characteristics:**
- Very fast turnaround required (same day)
- Higher volume, lower price per image
- Less emphasis on "artistic style"
- More standardized editing needs
