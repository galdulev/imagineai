## Research Quality Assurance

### Validation Methods
1. **Cross-source validation** - Verify findings across multiple sources
2. **Pattern consistency** - Ensure patterns hold across different data sets
3. **Logical consistency** - Check for contradictions and resolve
4. **Source credibility** - Assess source reliability
5. **Bias identification** - Recognize and account for potential biases

### Confidence Scoring
- **High confidence** - Multiple reliable sources, consistent patterns
- **Medium confidence** - Some sources, reasonable patterns
- **Low confidence** - Limited sources, needs validation
- **Hypothesis** - Educated guess, needs testing

## Limitations & Considerations

### AI Research Limitations
1. **Data availability** - Limited to publicly available information
2. **Real-time data** - May not have latest updates
3. **User privacy** - Cannot access private user data
4. **Direct observation** - Cannot observe actual user behavior
5. **Validation needed** - Some findings may need user validation

### When to Supplement with Traditional Research
- User interviews for deep qualitative insights
- Usability testing for direct observation
- Surveys for quantitative validation
- Analytics for actual usage data
- A/B testing for validation
