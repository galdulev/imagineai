AI Mobile Gallery

A mobile-first AI gallery that automatically segments weddings, recognizes faces, presents beautifully on mobile, and includes built-in monetization.

AI Mobile Gallery

Feature List & Why It Matters
1. AI Story-Based Segmentation
What:
Automatically groups photos into chapters (Getting Ready, Ceremony, Couple Portraits, Party, etc.)
Why:
Photographers spend hours organizing manually. Creates cinematic "wedding narrative" and removes the most time-consuming, unpaid part of workflow.
2. Face Recognition + "Find Me"
What:
Guests upload selfie or tap face cluster to find all photos they appear in
Why:
Solves #1 annoyance after delivery ("can you send photos of us?") and gives guests magical self-service.
3. Mobile-First Premium Experience
What:
Optimized for mobile (90% of guests view on phones), fast loading, intuitive scrolling, gorgeous lightbox
Why:
Today's galleries are desktop-era tools painfully viewed on phones. Mobile-native UX makes photos shine and creates beautiful branded experience.
4. Photographer Branding Built In
What:
Logo, name, brand color, subtle "Book me" link inside gallery
Why:
Every guest becomes a potential client. Free marketing without feeling like selling.
5. Smart Monetization
What:
Tip button, buy prints, one-click "request an album" (AI-selected best photos)
Why:
Photographers hate selling. Makes money flow passively, turning galleries from liability into revenue source.
6. One-Click Gallery Creation
What:
CTA appears after editing: "Create AI Gallery"
Why:
No extra tools, no exports, no uploads. Finished edits ‚Üí published gallery in minutes, not hours.
7. Privacy & Access Controls
What:
Password, PIN, public link, or guest-only mode
Why:
Different cultures and events have different sensitivity levels. Protects photographers and clients while keeping workflows flexible.
8. High-Quality Display + Downloads
What:
Web-optimized thumbnails, full-resolution downloads (if allowed), watermarks (optional)
Why:
Screenshots destroy quality and brand visibility. High-quality downloads protect the art and reputation.
9. Gallery Analytics Dashboard
What:
Viewers, unique visitors, downloads, favorites, revenue generated
Why:
Photographers rarely see how widely their work is shared. Analytics helps understand performance and justify pricing.
10. Easy Sharing + QR Code
What:
Auto-generated share link + QR code for couples
Why:
Wedding galleries spread across WhatsApp, iMessage, Instagram. Sharing must be frictionless for viral exposure.
11. Favorites System for Couples
What:
Couples can select favorites and export lists
Why:
They typically send messy screenshots to photographers. Favorites create organized workflow for albums and prints.
12. Offline-Ready Caching
What:
Prefetching and lightweight caching on mobile
Why:
Guests often view photos with weak reception (venues, hotels). Smoothness is critical to perceived quality and brand trust.
13. Photographer Control Panel
What:
Reorder segments, rename sections, hide or spotlight specific photos
Why:
AI does the heavy lifting but photographers stay in control ‚Äî builds trust and reduces friction.
14. Multi-Event Management
What:
Each event has its own gallery with status (Draft / Published)
Why:
Photographers juggle many clients at once. They need clarity at a glance.
15. Push Notifications
What:
Real-time notifications for gallery views, client shares, favorites, and engagement
Why:
Photographers want to know when clients engage with their work. Notifications create awareness and enable timely follow-ups, turning passive galleries into active client touchpoints.
16. Mobile Culling/Editing
What:
AI-powered culling and basic editing capabilities accessible from mobile devices
Why:
Enables photographers to review, cull, and make quick edits on-the-go, extending workflow flexibility beyond the desktop. Critical for photographers who need to work from multiple locations or during travel.
