Part 1: Business Goal & User Segmentation

From "Tool" to "Operating System"

The Business Goal: Maximize Share of Workflow

Our business goal is not just to sell more edits. It is to maximize Share of Workflow.

Current State: Imagen AI is a powerful tool that photographers use for specific tasks (culling & editing).
Target State: Imagen AI becomes the operating system for photographers' entire workflow‚Äîfrom first client inquiry to final delivery and beyond.

Why "Share of Workflow" Matters

1. Retention

The more of a photographer's workflow we own, the harder it is to switch

2. Revenue

More touchpoints = more opportunities for value creation and monetization

3. Differentiation

Competitors can copy features, but they can't copy deep workflow integration

4. Mission Alignment

"Photographers one-stop-shop" requires owning more than just editing

The Mobile Experience Gap: Critical Analysis

What Problem or Inefficiency Are They Facing Without a Mobile Experience?

1. No On-Site Workflow Integration (Highest Impact)

  • Lost Time: Can't start culling during breaks (30-60 min lost per wedding)
  • Delayed Feedback: Can't preview/edit on-site to catch issues early
  • No Real-Time Backup: Can't verify backup integrity immediately
  • Client Communication: Can't share previews during day for "wow" moment

Impact: Workflow starts only after returning home (8-12 hour delay). Can't provide "sneak peek" during wedding day.

2. No Mobile Review Capability (High Impact)

  • Location Constraint: Tied to desktop computer
  • Time Waste: Can't use travel time productively
  • Delayed Delivery: Must wait until at computer to finalize

3. No Client Preview System (Medium-High Impact)

  • Missed Opportunity: Can't provide "sneak peek" experience
  • No Early Feedback: Can't catch client preferences early
  • Delayed Communication: Client sees nothing until full delivery

Strategic Value:

  • Expands Share of Workflow: From 36% to 45%+ (adding shoot day)
  • Competitive Differentiation: No competitor offers mobile workflow
  • Daily Engagement: Mobile = daily touchpoint vs. weekly desktop
  • Retention: Mobile apps have 2-3x higher retention than web-only
