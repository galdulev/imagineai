## Executive Summary

Imagen AI is a **B2C SaaS photo editing platform** serving **professional photographers** who need to process high volumes of images efficiently while maintaining their unique editing style. The platform has **19,000+ community members**, processes **150+ million photos annually**, and raised **$30M in Series A funding** (December 2022).

**Primary ICP:** Wedding and event photographers who process 500-5,000+ images per event and spend 3-16 hours editing per wedding.
