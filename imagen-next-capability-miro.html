‚òÖ Research Principles
1. Problem First, Solution Second
Avoid solutioning before deeply understanding user pain. The capability emerges from the research.
2. Triangulate Data Sources
No single data point should drive decisions. Combine quantitative analytics, qualitative interviews, and behavioral experiments.
3. Test Cheap Before Building Expensive
Fake doors and painted doors before engineering. The goal is to fail fast and learn cheap.
4. Segment the Research
Wedding photographers ‚â† Portrait photographers ‚â† Commercial photographers. Each segment has different needs.
5. Measure Behavior, Not Just Opinions
What users do > What users say. Behavioral evidence trumps stated preferences every time.
6. Set Kill Criteria Upfront
Define what failure looks like before we're emotionally invested. Know when to pivot or kill an idea.
